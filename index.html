Obstacle Odyssey: Evade and Excel

Background

Welcome to Obstacle Odyssey! In a world filled with twists and turns, your goal is simple: dodge obstacles and reach the finish line. Journey through various landscapes, from ancient ruins to futuristic cities, each brimming with challenges. Navigate through spinning gears, dodge fiery traps, and leap over barriers to progress. Sharpen your reflexes and test your skills as you aim to conquer each level, all while uncovering the mysteries hidden within this adventurous realm. Are you ready to dodge, weave, and triumph in the face of obstacles?

How to play

Press your mouse anywhere within the frame of the game to start playing. Move the mouse to move the block and avoid obstacles. Click the mouse to speed up the flow of obstacles.
