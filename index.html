Obstacle Odyssey: Evade and Excel

Background

Welcome to Obstacle Odyssey! In a world filled with twists and turns, your goal is simple: dodge obstacles and reach the finish line. Navigate through the obstacles to progress with the goal of sharpening your reflexes.

How to play

Press your mouse anywhere within the frame of the game to start playing. Move the mouse to move the block and avoid obstacles. Click the mouse to speed up the flow of obstacles.
